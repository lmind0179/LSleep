### 🌙 Sleep quietly tonight
**LSleep** is a premium sleep audio guide designed to provide a deep and uninterrupted night's rest using only **7 meticulously designed calming sleep sounds** and a **precision sleep timer** system—completely free of clunky interfaces and intrusive ads.

Once you fall asleep, LSleep gently fades out and fully terminates the audio session, protecting both your hearing and your device's battery life.

### 🔬 Scientific Audio Engine

#### 1. Phantom Bass™ Technology
Due to structural constraints, smartphone speakers cannot natively reproduce deep frequencies below 150Hz. **LSleep** utilizes psychoacoustic perception principles to synthesize **deep sleep low-frequencies between 50Hz and 150Hz** without causing distortion.
* **Fundamental Frequency Reconstruction:** Synthesizes harmonics to allow your brain to perceive rich, non-existent low-frequencies.
* **8th-Order Low-Pass Filter (LPF):** Eliminates harsh high frequencies, retaining only the calming and restorative frequencies.
* **Loudness Safety:** Keeps listening levels uniform and gentle to prevent acoustic shock.

#### 2. 3-Phase Journey Engine
An intelligent audio engine that morphs the frequency filter characteristics dynamically to match your natural sleep progression:
1. **Phase 1: Onset [Masking 250Hz]** - Blocks external noise and distracting thoughts with comfortable masking frequencies to help you drift off quickly.
2. **Phase 2: Deep** - Gradually amplifies deep low-frequencies to encourage deep, slow-wave sleep.
3. **Phase 3: Rest [Morphic Protector 100Hz]** - Locks into a stable 100Hz low-frequency filter state to preserve a quiet environment and protect your restorative rest.
